Bang Talat (Thai: บางตลาด, pronounced [bāːŋ tā.làːt]) is one of the twelve subdistricts (tambon) of Pak Kret district, in Nonthaburi province, Thailand. Neighbouring subdistricts are (from north clockwise) Pak Kret, Khlong Kluea, Thung Song Hong, Tha Sai and Tha It and Ko Kret. In 2020, it had a total population of 47,147 people.
