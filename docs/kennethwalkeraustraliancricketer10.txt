Kenneth Walker (born 25 June 1941) is an Australian former cricketer. He played one first-class cricket match for Victoria in 1962.
A short-statured left-arm seam bowler, Walker played 92 district cricket matches for Northcote and six for St Kilda, taking 258 career wickets at 20.01.
